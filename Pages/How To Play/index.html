Classes

There are many different classes to choose from at a beginner level:

  • Soldier(AK): Full auto weapon, 100HP
  • Hitman (Sniper): Semi auto action, 90HP
  • Gunner (SMG): Full auto, 90HP
  • Heavy (LMG): Full auto, 150 HP, slow reload time
  • Rocketeer (RPG): Bolt action, 90HP
  • Agent (MP5): Full auto, 100HP
  • Brawler (M37): Full auto, 120HP
  • Investor (Scar): Semi auto, 80HP
  • Assassin (Famas): Burst action, 110HP

The remaining amount of classes can be unlocked with special requirements, such as Juggernaut(100 Kills), Vanguard(200 kills), etc.

Maps

There are multiple maps to choose from that are a part of the Kour experience (Kour HQ, Havana, Snowstorm, Ghost town, Kour House, etc.). Each map can be used for multiple modes.

Modes

  • Free For All - Users are all opponents of each other. Most points wins the round.
  • Hardpoint - Two teams defend the a checkpoint on rotation throughout the map. Whichever team stays on point longer, wins.
  • TDM - Two teams, Terrorists and Counter Terrorists compete to see which side gets more kills and points.
  • Kourstrike - Players can experience a CS:GO gaming experience including timed demolition, parkour, etc.
  • Gun Game - Players spawn in a FFA lobby and after each kill, they rotate to the next weapon. Winner is decided based off who goes through the entire rotation first.
  • Kourzone - A battle-royale version of Kour, bringing a survival-gaming experience to players.
  • Parkour - A game mode centered around movement, Surfing and doing complex jumps and maneuvers to get to the end.
  • MoonSnipe - Low gravity gravity shooter mode with shotgun jumping, ray guns, and the ability to dash when you press E.

Skins

Skins can be unboxed in crates inside of Kour.io within the "Shop" icon on the left tab. Through this feature, players can use KP (Kour Points) to unbox crates and get a randomly selected prize, differing in rarity. Players can get one free crate if they watch an ad to support Kour.io.

Mobile

Kour.io, on mobile, can be played from browser or downloaded on the Google Play store.
